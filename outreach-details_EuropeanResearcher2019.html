  • Title: European Researchers' Night
  • Publication date: September, 2019
  • Link: www.example.com

Description:

The European Researchers' Night is a Europe-spanning public event that showcases the many facets of science and its profound influence on everyday life, all while making it an engaging and enjoyable experience. In the 2019 edition, I, along with my colleagues from the Proaction Lab, actively participated in this event, offering a range of hands-on activities tailored for individuals of all ages, including children.
